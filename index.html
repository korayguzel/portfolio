I graduated in Molecular Biology and Genetics from Istanbul Technical University. During my master's studies at Acıbadem University, I worked on aging research with long-lived cell models and developed a stronger pull toward computational analysis.

At Rizzoli Lab, University Medicine Göttingen, I analyzed proteomic and transcriptomic data on brain aging, performed immunofluorescence staining and cryostat sectioning, and built R/Bioconductor pipelines with tools such as DESeq2 and WGCNA.

I later left the master's program to pursue independent research and tool building. I use AI-assisted coding heavily, but the real value I bring is biological judgment, problem framing, literature review, and validating whether the tool is scientifically useful.

ProtQC

Physics-based quality control for AI-designed proteins

Open GitHub

A practical validation pipeline for generated protein structures that combines molecular dynamics, cavity detection, hydrogen-bond persistence, secondary structure stability, SASA checks, and a composite PASS/WARNING/FAIL verdict.

Output CLI workflow + FastQC-style HTML reports
